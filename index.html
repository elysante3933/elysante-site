About Elysanté

Elysanté is not for everyone. This is a space reserved for those who understand the meaning of discretion, respect each other's time, and return attention rather than demand it.

❌ Not recommended for:

  • Those who cannot behave with courtesy and respect,
  • Those who initiate contact with only “Hi”, “Hey”, or a single emoji – such messages will not be answered,
  • Those who treat the experience as a transaction rather than a meeting,
  • Those who do not accept that Elysanté is not a conveyor belt, but a personal and exclusive space – unavailable without prior arrangement,
  • Those who hide their identity or refuse to present themselves (a photo, age, and contact information are mandatory for all first introductions).

Elysanté is a world of its own – and like true luxury, it does not tolerate disrespect, impatience, or superficiality.

Private, By Invitation Only – "Where Comfort Meets Luxury"

For some, true comfort begins where they feel at home – a refined hotel suite, a quiet apartment, a penthouse above the city. Elysanté arrives discreetly to your chosen place, where everything happens at your pace. This visit is not just an appointment, but a personalised gesture – in the spirit of true attention and discretion.

Program Name: "Velvet Voyage" – The Traveling Experience

The goal of the program is to allow the guest to enjoy a refined, sensual indulgence in their own familiar environment. A complete relaxing treatment that combines silky touch, the calming power of aromas, and the art of attentive presence.

  • 60-minute full body massage (with lavender & rose oil)
  • Personalised fragrance mist for the room
  • Soft background music set via Bluetooth speaker
  • Exclusive welcome tea or fresh juice
  • Delicate hand care at the end of the session
  • Personal, quiet closing to ease into the evening calm
